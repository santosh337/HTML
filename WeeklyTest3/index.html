How did Air Jordan start?

The first Air Jordan shoe was produced for basketball
player Michael Jordan during his time with the Chicago
Bulls in late 1984 and released to the public on April 1,
1985. The shoes were designed for Nike by Peter Moore,
Tinker Hatfield, and Bruce Kilgore.
